Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how the uTrack Sports mobile app (“uTrack,” “the app,” “we,” “us”) handles information. We built uTrack to keep your team's data simple and under your control. Please read this policy alongside the app's listing on the Apple App Store.

The short version

  • The team data you enter — teams, players, games, and statistics — is stored locally on your device. We do not run a server that collects or stores it.
  • uTrack does not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment details.
  • We use analytics and crash-reporting tools to understand how the app is used and to fix problems. These collect limited, app-related diagnostic information, described below.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Information stored on your device

When you use uTrack, you create content such as team names, rosters, player details, game records, and the statistics you track during games. This content is stored locally on your device using the app's on-device database. It is not transmitted to or stored by us.

If you choose to use a device or operating-system backup feature (for example, an iCloud device backup), your app data may be included in that backup according to Apple's terms and your device settings. That backup is controlled by you and Apple, not by uTrack.

Information you choose to share

uTrack lets you export and share game summaries and statistics (for example as PDF, HTML, or CSV) using your device's standard sharing options — such as email or messaging. When you share a file, it goes only to the recipients and services you select. uTrack does not send these files to us.

Analytics and crash reporting

To improve the app and diagnose problems, uTrack uses third-party analytics and crash-reporting services, including Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics). These tools may collect limited information such as:

  • App usage and events (for example, which screens or features are used), in aggregate;
  • Device and app information (such as device model, operating-system version, app version, language/region, and a mobile-app or device identifier);
  • Crash and diagnostic data that helps us find and fix bugs.

This information is used to understand overall app usage and stability. It is not used to identify you personally, and the team data you enter (players, games, stats) is not sent to these services. Google processes this data as described in Firebase's privacy and security documentation and the Google Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions and purchases

uTrack offers an optional premium subscription. Purchases and renewals are handled entirely by the Apple App Store using your Apple ID. uTrack receives confirmation of your subscription status so it can unlock premium features, but we do not receive or store your name, card number, or other payment information. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's privacy policy and terms.

Children's privacy

uTrack is designed for coaches, team managers, and parents who track youth sports. It is intended for use by adults. While the app may contain information about youth players that an adult chooses to enter, that information is stored locally on the adult user's device as described above. uTrack does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

How information is used

  • To provide the app's core features — tracking, organizing, and reporting on games and players;
  • To process and recognize your subscription through Apple;
  • To understand app usage in aggregate and improve features;
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and technical problems.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. Limited diagnostic and analytics information is processed by our service providers (such as Google Firebase) solely to provide the analytics and crash-reporting functions described above. We may disclose information if required by law.

Your choices

  • Your data: Because team data is stored on your device, you can remove it by deleting items in the app or by deleting the app, which removes its locally stored data from that device.
  • Device-level controls: Your device's settings let you manage permissions, advertising/identifier settings, and backups.
  • Subscriptions: You can manage or cancel a subscription in your Apple ID / App Store settings.

Data security

Your team data stays on your device, which reduces exposure compared with apps that store everything on a central server. Any data transmitted by analytics and crash-reporting tools is sent over standard encrypted connections. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves — for example, if we add or change features or service providers. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes may also be noted in the app or its App Store listing.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your information, contact us at support@utracksports.com.
