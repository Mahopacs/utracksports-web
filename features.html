Features

Everything you need to
keep the book

uTrack was built around one job: capturing a youth game accurately, fast, from the sideline — soccer or basketball — then handing you clean stats afterward.

⏱️

A real running clock

Start, pause, and run the clock by period — counting up or down. uTrack timestamps plays as they happen, so your timeline and minutes-played come out right without extra bookkeeping.

⏳

Player minutes & subs

Swap players in and out from the bench, and uTrack tracks who's on the field or court and tallies minutes played for every player automatically.

±

Plus/minus on every player

uTrack tracks your team's scoring margin while each player is on — the impact number a plain box score never shows, for soccer and basketball alike.

👤

Tap-to-track stat entry

Your lineup is right there. Tap a player and log a stat in a couple of taps — on the soccer field or the basketball court. Built to keep pace with a live game.

📊

Reports that build themselves

A live box score, game reports, a full-season record, and a play-by-play feed — all generated from the plays you logged. No spreadsheet afterward.

📤

Share with the team

Send a game summary or stats to parents and players as PDF, HTML, or CSV, straight from your phone using the share options you already use.

🗃️

Teams, players, leagues & seasons

Set up rosters with numbers and starter/sub status, group teams into leagues, and keep full seasons of history in one place.

📱

On your device, no account

Your team data lives locally on your phone. There's no sign-up wall — install it and start tracking.

Soccer

Soccer, tracked in full

The deepest part of a soccer game is everything that happens away from the ball. uTrack tracks all of it.

⚽

21 trackable stats

Log 21 different soccer stats as they happen — well past goals and assists, into fouls, corners, tackles, offsides and more. Track exactly the ones you care about.

🧤

Full goalkeeper stats

Goalkeepers get their own stat line: saves, goals allowed, and blocks — shots on goal stopped. A category plenty of apps skip entirely.

📊

The game timeline & reports

A visual match timeline, game statistics, a full box score, and play-by-play — the complete report set for every soccer game you track.

Basketball

Basketball, with the shot chart up front

Track where the game is won — every shot, every possession, every player's impact.

🏀

Every shot, mapped

Tap where each shot came from as it happens, and uTrack turns those taps into a full shot chart of the game — makes and misses, right on the floor.

🎯

The shot chart is free

See the complete shot chart with no paywall — viewing it is free. Premium only comes in if you want to share or export it.

📊

The full stat set, live

Points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks, turnovers, fouls, shooting splits and more — logged live, with plus/minus and minutes on every player.
