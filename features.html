Features

Everything you need to
keep the book

uTrack was built around one job: capturing a youth sports game accurately, fast, from the sideline — then handing you clean stats afterward.

⚽

Tap-to-track stat entry

The field shows your lineup. Tap a player and log a goal, assist, shot, save, foul, corner, or card in a couple of taps. Built to keep pace with a live match instead of slowing you down.

⏱️

A real match clock

Start, pause, and run the clock by period. uTrack timestamps plays as they happen, so your timeline and minutes-played come out right without extra bookkeeping.

🔄

Substitutions & minutes

Swap players in and out from the bench. uTrack tracks who's on the field and tallies minutes played per player automatically.

↺

Undo, for the sideline

Logged the wrong player or a goal got called back? Undo with a confirmation step. Games are messy — the app expects corrections.

📊

Reports that build themselves

Game statistics, a match timeline, a full box score, and a play-by-play feed — all generated from the plays you logged. No spreadsheet afterward.

📤

Share with the team

Send a game summary or stats to parents and players as PDF, HTML, or CSV, straight from your phone using the share options you already use.

🗃️

Teams, players, leagues & seasons

Set up rosters with numbers and starter/sub status, group teams into leagues, and keep full seasons of history in one place.

📱

On your device, no account

Your team data lives locally on your phone. There's no sign-up wall — install it and start tracking.

Try before the whistle

There's a demo game built right in

Every install includes a finished demo match so you can explore the stats, timeline, and reports before your first real game.
