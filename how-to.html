How it works

Track a game in five steps

You don't need to be a stats person. If you can tap a player when something happens, you can keep a full book.

Set up your team

Add your team and roster — player names, numbers, and who's starting. Group teams into a league or season if you want to keep history organized.

Start a game

Create a game against your opponent, set your starting lineup, and you're at the field view with both sides laid out and the clock ready.

Track from the sideline

Run the clock and tap players as plays happen — goals, assists, shots, saves, fouls, corners, cards. Make substitutions from the bench. Tap undo if something needs fixing.

Read the stats

At full-time, open the game statistics, match timeline, box score, or play-by-play — all built automatically from what you logged.

Share it

Send the summary and stats to parents and players as a PDF, HTML, or CSV file using your phone's normal sharing — email, messages, wherever your team lives.

Not sure yet? Start with the demo.

Every install of uTrack comes with a finished demo match already loaded. Open it to explore the live screen, stats, timeline, and reports with real data before you track your first game.
