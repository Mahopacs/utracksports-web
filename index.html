Why uTrack

Built for the person actually
keeping the book

Not a social network. Not a streaming service. A fast, focused tool for tracking what actually happens on the field and court — and turning it into reports you can use.

⏱️

A real running clock

Start the clock, make your subs, and uTrack figures out how long each player was on the field or court — automatically. No separate stopwatch, no math.

⏳

Player minutes, automatic

Swap players from the bench and uTrack tallies minutes played for every one of them — straight from your subs, no bookkeeping.

±

Plus/minus on every player

See your team’s scoring margin while each player is on the floor or field — the impact number a plain box score never shows.

👤

Tap-to-track

Log every stat in a couple of taps on the live game view. Soccer: tap the player, then the stat. Basketball: pick the stat, then the player. Fast enough to keep up with a real game.

📤

Share in a tap

Send game summaries and stats to parents and players as PDF, HTML, CSV, or Excel — straight from your phone. Sharing is a premium feature; families never pay to view stats.

📱

Yours, on your device

Your team data lives on your phone. No account to create just to start keeping stats.

Soccer

Soccer, tracked in full

Log 21 different soccer stats live — the whole game, not just goals and assists. Goalkeepers get their own stat line too: saves, goals allowed, and blocks. Then let the game timeline, box score, and full-season team and player reports build themselves.

Basketball

Basketball, with the shot chart

Tap exactly where every shot comes from and uTrack builds a full shot chart of the game — makes and misses, right on the floor. The shot chart is a premium feature and the one basketball coaches tell us changes how they think about their offense. Log the complete stat set live — points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks and more — with plus/minus and minutes on every player.
