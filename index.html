Why uTrack

Built for the person actually
keeping the book

⏱️

A real running clock

A game clock that runs like the scoreboard — counts up or down by period and timestamps every play, so minutes played and the game timeline come out right on their own.

⏳

Player minutes, automatic

Swap players from the bench and uTrack tallies minutes played for every one of them — straight from your subs, no stopwatch, no bookkeeping.

±

Plus/minus on every player

See your team's scoring margin while each player is on the floor or field — the impact number a plain box score never shows.

👤

Tap-to-track

Log every stat in a couple of taps on the live game view. Soccer: tap the player, then the stat. Basketball: pick the stat, then the player. Fast enough to keep up with a real game.

📤

Share in a tap

Send game summaries and stats to parents and players as PDF, HTML, or CSV — straight from your phone.

📱

Yours, on your device

Your team data lives on your phone. No account to create just to start keeping stats.

Soccer

Soccer, tracked in full

Log 21 different soccer stats live — the whole game, not just goals and assists. Goalkeepers get their own stat line too: saves, goals allowed, and blocks. Then let the game timeline, box score, and full-season team and player reports build themselves.

Basketball

Basketball, with the shot chart

Tap exactly where every shot comes from and uTrack builds a full shot chart of the game — free to view, no wall. Log the complete stat set live — points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks and more — with plus/minus and minutes on every player.

Set up unlimited teams, players, games and seasons — no caps, ever. Run the whole live game: full score, a running clock, substitutions, and every stat logged as it happens. You always see a real box score with the core stats, free. A genuine stat book, at no cost.

Premium — the whole story

You log everything for free — premium unlocks the reports that turn your live tracking into a record worth keeping and sharing. It fills in the full box score with the columns you choose, the deeper game reports, and full-season team and player reports — then shares or exports any of it as PDF, HTML, or Excel.

Every install includes 30 days of full premium access. After that you're on the free plan with the core reports — premium is an optional App Store subscription, priced in the app.
