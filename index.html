Why uTrack

Built for the person actually
keeping the book

⚽

Tap-to-track

Log goals, assists, shots, saves, cards and more by tapping players on a live field view. Fast enough to keep up with a real match.

⏱️

Live game screen

Period and clock controls, substitutions, score, and an undo for when a play gets called back. The sideline is messy — the app expects it.

📊

Box scores & reports

Per-player and per-game stats roll up automatically into clean reports — game stats, timeline, box score, and play-by-play.

📤

Share in a tap

Send game summaries and stats to parents and players as PDF, HTML, or CSV — straight from your phone.

🗃️

Teams, players, seasons

Organize rosters, leagues, and full seasons. Your history stays with you, game after game.

📱

Yours, on your device

Your team data lives on your phone. No account to create just to start keeping stats.
